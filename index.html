NEGATIVE IMPACTS ON ONE'S HEALTH

Digestive System

When junk food is more salty or sugary, it will cause high blood pressure. Moreover, there is a high chances of getting diabetes.

Respiratory System

Excess calories cause weight gain and increase the risk of respiratory diseases such as asthma and difficulty breathing.

Central Nervous System

Because junk foods are high in saturated fats and Omega-6 fatty acids, individuals are likely to get depressed.

Reproductive System

Phthalates can increase the risk of birth defects in the fetus.

Bones and Teeth

Carbs, sugars in fast foods and processed foods can increase the acid in the mouth that breaks down tooth enamel, causing tooth decay.

Fast food is significantly harmful to everyone's health, it not only makes people lose shape but also causes a number of dangerous diseases.
